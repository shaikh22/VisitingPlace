OOTY

Summers in Ooty are not on an extreme hot and humid side like the rest of south India. The temperature in summers, on an average remains between 20°C and 30°C. Days are a little hot but the heat is bearable. One can go out for sightseeing at Avalanche Lake, Botanical Garden and Ooty Lake in the evenings. The nights are cooler and pleasant. Also known as the Queen of Hills, Ooty is a scenic town which is surrounded by the beautiful Nilgiri Hills. The place has a beautiful climate all round the year because of its perfect location. The winters in Ooty are colder than rest of the south India. The best time to visit this beautiful hill station is from October to June. In these months, the temperature remains somewhere around 25°C which offers a pleasant weather. It is the best time for traveling and sightseeing. During these months, nights are also comfortable.
Ooty is the perfect place to spend some fun filled moments away from the hectic city life. With a pleasant weather throughout the year, Ooty is one place that can be visited throughout the year. Known as the Summer Capital of the Madras Presidency, Ooty attracts visitors especially during the summer season. Ooty in summer looks splendid and definitely worth visiting by everyone. Ooty offers a number of sightseeing options for tourists visiting this place. Ooty lake, Rose Garden, Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta peak, Ketti valley, Pykara river, Pine forest, Toda huts, Mudumalai and Mukurthi National Parks are some of the must visit tourist attractions of Ooty.
