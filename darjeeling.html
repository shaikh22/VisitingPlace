DARJEELING

Darjeeling is one of the most well-known hill stations of India, The pleasant and soothing weather adds to the ethereal charm of the place and makes it more alluring. No wonder, the best time to visit Darjeeling is throughout the year. The weather remains cool which is ideal for sightseeing of various attractions. However, it is best to avoid the monsoon season as landslides are common during this period. Here is a season wise break up which will help you plan your Darjeeling tour for cherishing perfect holidays. While summer months from April to June are the ideal time to travel to Darjeeling, some travellers including couples also prefer to visit the beautiful town during winter months from October to December.
