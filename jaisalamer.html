JAISALMER

Jaisalmer, the ‘Golden City’, is known for its sandstone architecture, ancient Jain temples, and massive walls that conjure up images of fables from the Arabian Nights. It is an ideal destination for a family vacation. The best time to visit Jaisalmer is between September and March. There are numerous fun things to do while holidaying with family in Jaisalmer. The city attracts tourists from all over the world and is especially famous for its camel safaris. Being an arid region in the middle of the Thar Desert means Jaisalmer’s weather can be extreme. Whether it is the hot months of summer or the freezing winters, temperatures can range from high to low. Winter is considered the best time to visit Jaisalmer; it is best to avoid the desert during the hot summer months.
The winter months last from November to March and this is the best time to visit Jaisalmer. You can enjoy some fun activities in the desert in very pleasant weather. The minimum temperature could dip to as low as 5 °C and the maximum could be 24 °C. The weather in January is enjoyable but can get a little too cold at times. For couples on their honeymoon, winter is the best time to visit. There are numerous romantic experiences on offer, such as camping under the night sky or in a secluded tent.
