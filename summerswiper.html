***PLACES TO VISIT DURING SUMMER***

1

LADAKH

Be it adventure, nature, tranquility or simply scenic views, this place has it all. The perfect destination in India during summers, this place is extremely harsh in terms of weather conditions and yet it tops the list all because of its exceptional beauty.Mountain passes, beautiful lakes, barren valleys, age-old Buddhist monasteries, trekking expeditions and motorcycle adventures, the place is a host to all.

2

MANALI

If it is summer in India, you cannot find a destination more aptly suitable for a vacation than Manali. If the picturesque views are the highlight of the region, the adventure sports in the area make it irresistible. But the list of attractions does not end here, the religious touch of the place with its temples and monasteries is a further mystifying feature of this place. The nearby Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass are some of the distinguished places one can visit while vacationing here.

3

SHIMLA

Shimla is an ideal destination to spend your summer holiday in India. It offers one of those home away from home experiences and draws attention of families who are looking for a comfortable, leisurely stay amidst picturesque surroundings. The reason I would pick Shimla as a family holiday destination is the fact that the climate is bearable in summers (neither too hot nor too cold), there are several avenues for dining (all cuisine are available), plentiful shopping and accommodation options, and the low-traffic.

4

KASHMIR

The word Kashmir makes people to think of snow and the melting Himalayas into lakes. The Valley is full of lakes and multicolored flora surrounding them. Take an amazing boat ride in Dal Lake, the waters reflecting the sun rays and colorful flowers during May. This is the most popular spot people opt for honeymoon and the first thought for a holiday visit in Summer.

Read More

5

MUSSOORIE

The Kempty Falls and Rajaji National Park are great sites to visit in Mussoorie during May. If you are planning a quiet time amidst all the busy tourist spots, then the Municipal Garden is the ideal place to stroll around or sit on soft grass surrounded by multi -colored flora. Originally it was known as ‘Company Bagh’ there is an artificial and miniature lake & waterfall inside. Spot wildlife at the Benog Wildlife Sanctury and take a ride across the skies slung on a rope at the Mussoorie Adventure Park.

6

TAWANG MONASTERY

At an altitude of over 2600 meters, Tawang, located near Arunachal Pradesh and the Tibetan border, is cool throughout the year with snow capped mountains in the backdrop. Dotted with hamlets and an awesome Buddhist monastery that houses a huge gilded statue of Lord Buddha, the place is the picture of tranquility. Popular with tourist from all over the country, visiting Tawang in the summer is ideal as weather conditions then make access and getting around the area easier. details of places

7

DARJEELING

The tea garden estates of Darjeeling are some of the most beautiful hills you will ever set your eyes on. A hot spot for tourism in the summer months thanks to the awesome weather here, the place is also a winter holiday destination though it can get quite chilly. Fun things to do here like riding the toy train and watching the sunrise at Tiger Hill are perfect activities in this magnificent part of West Bengal.

8

RANIKHET

A little hill station cantonment area, Ranikhet is also called the Queens Meadow on account of the fact that this is believed to be one of Queen Padmini of Kumaoni residences. Steeped in ancient history and adorned with beautiful monuments, not to mention the natural magnificence and wonderful climate, makes this town a top contender for summer relief in Uttarakhand.

9

NAINITAL

Merely four hours from Delhi by road, Nainital is a beautiful summer holiday destination, surrounded by rolling hills and blessed with great weather year round. A favourite place for people from Delhi and Chandigarh to visit when the heat gets too much in the capital, Nainital is always bustling with tourists. Even the winter months here are great as the countryside is equally spectacular during this time of year.

10

Coonoor

Coonoor is the second largest hill station in Tamilnadu and 50 km. away from Ooty. This is another quiet pasture of the tea gardens. This hill station is even more peaceful and quite than Ooty making it another perfect spot to cool off the summer heat in May. This is one of the best places to visit in India during May. Take a ride on the Heritage train to get the best views of picturesque hills & nature side.
